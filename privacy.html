1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, password, and company name when you register.
  • Company profile: Business name, phone number, address, license number, specialty, and service area that you enter in your profile settings.
  • Business data: Bids, invoices, contracts, client records, CRM leads, change orders, and marketing content you create within the platform.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed by Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers — Stripe handles all payment data under their own privacy policy.
  • Communications: Any messages you send to our support team.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, buttons clicked, time spent in the app, and error logs.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address.
  • Cookies: Session cookies required to keep you logged in, and optional analytics cookies described in Section 7.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the BuildIQ platform
  • Generate AI-powered bids, contracts, invoices, and marketing content using your company profile and project details
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, password resets, account alerts)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the product
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information or use it to train AI models.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers

We share data with third-party vendors who help us operate the platform:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Your company profile and project details are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate documents. See Section 4.
  • Stripe: Payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Authentication and user management.
  • Railway: Cloud hosting provider where the application runs.

Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of BuildIQ, our users, or the public.

Business transfers

If BuildIQ is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email before such a transfer occurs.

4. AI Processing & Claude AI

Plain English: When you generate a bid, contract, or marketing piece, we send your project details and company profile to Anthropic's Claude AI. Anthropic processes this data under their enterprise privacy policy and does not use it to train their models.

BuildIQ uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate documents and content. When you trigger an AI generation, we transmit:

  • Your company name, specialty, years of experience, and service area
  • The project details, scope, and specifications you provide for that generation
  • Any additional context you include in the prompt fields

We do not send: your email address, billing details, client contact information, or raw invoice/contract data to Anthropic.

Anthropic's processing is governed by their Privacy Policy and their API usage terms, which prohibit using API inputs to train models.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your account:

  • You may export your data before cancellation
  • Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation to allow recovery
  • After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted from our systems
  • Stripe retains payment records as required by financial regulations

You can request immediate deletion of your data by contacting us at the address in Section 11.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Passwords stored as cryptographic hashes (never in plain text)
  • Database access restricted to authorized application services
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you become aware of any security issue, please contact us immediately at tyler@bruce-building.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Essential cookies (always active)

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your preferences. These are required for the app to function and cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies (optional)

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the platform. These cookies collect anonymous usage data (pages visited, features used) and do not identify you personally. You can opt out of analytics by adjusting your browser cookie settings.

No advertising cookies

We do not use advertising tracking cookies or share your data with ad networks for behavioral advertising purposes.

8. Your Rights

  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data (most data can be updated directly in your account settings)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tyler@bruce-building.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact us at the email above.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing your data is the performance of a contract (providing the Service you signed up for). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
