Common questions

Straight answers

Do I need to know anything about AI to use BuildIQ? +
No. You fill out a form about your project, click a button, and BuildIQ handles the rest. If you can send a text message, you can use BuildIQ. The AI is working behind the scenes — you just see the finished document.
Are the AI-generated contracts legally binding? +
BuildIQ generates professional contract templates based on standard construction contract law. They're comprehensive and well-structured — but like any contract template, we recommend having a licensed attorney in your state review before signing, especially for large projects. Every contract we generate includes this disclaimer clearly.
How long does it take to generate a bid? +
About 10-20 minutes total. Most of that is you filling in the project details. The actual AI generation takes under 30 seconds. The more detail you provide in the project scope field, the more accurate and specific the bid will be.
Can I edit the content Claude generates? +
Yes. Everything Claude generates is a starting point. You can copy the text and adjust any numbers, terms, or language before sending. We're adding inline editing and version history in the next release.
What happens to my data? +
Your business data (clients, bids, invoices, contracts) is stored securely in our database and is never sold or shared. Project details sent to Claude AI are processed under Anthropic's enterprise privacy policy and are not used to train their models.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, no questions asked. Cancel from your account settings at any time and you won't be charged again. You keep access through the end of your billing period and can export all your data.
Does BuildIQ work on mobile? +
The web app works on mobile browsers. A dedicated iOS and Android app is on our roadmap for Q3 2026, which will let you create bids on-site from your phone or tablet.
